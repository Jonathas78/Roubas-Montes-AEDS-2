## <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f913/512.gif" alt="ü§ì" width="27px"> Explanation
  1. Each round, a card is drawn.
  
  2. If your card matches one in the discard pile, you stack both cards together. 
  
  3. If it matches the top of your stack, you place it on top of your stack. 
  
  4. If it matches the top of another player‚Äôs stack, you steal their stack and place the drawn card on top.
